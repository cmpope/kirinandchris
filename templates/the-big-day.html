Ceremony & Reception

The ceremony and reception will be held at Holman Ranch in Carmel Valley, California. The ceremony will begin at 4pm, so it is requested that you arrive on the property by 3:30pm.

Upon arrival, go through the entrance gate and follow the single lane road past the stables. An attendant will indicate where to park and your group will be shuttled up to the ceremony site.

Following the reception, the bride and groom invite you to join them and their families for drinks and late-night bites at Will’s Fargo Steakhouse.

Please be prepared to be outdoors for most of the evening.
